# Gamma distribution density parameterised with mean and standard deviation

Gamma distribution density parameterised with mean and standard
deviation

## Usage

``` r
rho(DAISIE_par, DAISIE_dist_pars)
```

## Arguments

- DAISIE_par:

  A numeric parameter to evaluate the integral of the function.

- DAISIE_dist_pars:

  A numeric vector of two elements, first is the mean and second the
  standard deviation of the distribution.

## Value

Numeric
